NewJeans 2nd EP 'Get Up'

'Get Up' showcases NewJeans' growth, featuring six tracks that explore themes of youth and romance.

About 'Get Up'

Released July 21, 2023, this EP contains tracks like 'Super Shy', 'New Jeans', and 'ETA'.

Track Highlights

'Super Shy' uses a liquid drum’n’bass base, while 'ETA' is funky and energetic.

Artistic Direction

The EP channels euphoria and personal growth through vibrant soundscapes.

Listener Impressions

The EP was praised for innovation and genre fluidity.
