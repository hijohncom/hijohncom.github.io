GOT7 / Present: YOU &ME Edition (2CD//Random Version)

Unveiling a heartfelt compilation, GOT7's 'Present: YOU &ME Edition' serves as a musical gift to their devoted fans.

Album Overview

Released on December 3, 2018, this repackage of the 'Present: YOU' album includes two CDs featuring the title track 'Miracle' and a collection of solo and unit songs. The album showcases the group's versatility and individual artistry, offering listeners a diverse auditory experience.

Critical Reception

Critics acknowledged the album's expansive tracklist and the inclusion of previously performed but unreleased songs. The title track 'Miracle' was praised for its emotive composition and the group's harmonious vocals.

Fan Reactions

Fans were elated with the abundance of new material and the opportunity to hear studio versions of concert favorites. The album was celebrated for its personal touch, with each member contributing to the songwriting and production process.
