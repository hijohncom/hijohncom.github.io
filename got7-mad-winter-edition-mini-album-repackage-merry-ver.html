GOT7 / MAD Winter Edition: Mini Album Repackage Merry Ver

Celebrating the holiday cheer, GOT7's 'MAD Winter Edition' in its Merry Version offers fans a festive musical experience.

Album Overview

This edition mirrors the Happy Version with its release date on November 23, 2015, and features the same tracklist, including 'Confession Song.' The Merry Version distinguishes itself with unique packaging and collectibles, catering to fans who appreciate variety.

Critical Reception

The album maintained the positive reception of its counterpart, with 'Confession Song' being highlighted for its sweet and endearing qualities. The packaging and additional items were also praised for enhancing the overall fan experience.

Fan Reactions

Enthusiasts enjoyed the seasonal theme and the group's charming delivery. The Merry Version's distinct packaging appealed to collectors and fans eager to own both versions. The festive concept was seen as a successful endeavor by GOT7 to connect with their audience during the holiday season.
