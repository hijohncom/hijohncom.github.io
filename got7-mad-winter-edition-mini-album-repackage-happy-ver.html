Embracing the festive spirit, GOT7's 'MAD Winter Edition' in its Happy Version brings warmth and joy to the holiday season.

Released on November 23, 2015, this repackage of the 'MAD' mini-album includes the title track 'Confession Song,' a sweet melody that captures winter emotions. The 'Confession Song' was noted for its adorable concept and relatable lyrics, resonating with listeners seeking a romantic winter tune. The overall presentation of the album was commended for its festive appeal and thoughtful inclusions.
