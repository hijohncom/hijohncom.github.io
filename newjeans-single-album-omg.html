NewJeans Single Album 'OMG'

'OMG' signals NewJeans' confident step forward with bold sonic experimentation and clean production.

Unveiling 'OMG'

Released January 2, 2023, this single album includes the tracks 'OMG' and 'Ditto'.

Musical Composition

'OMG' blends R&B with hip-hop sensibilities while 'Ditto' delivers a softer, emotional vibe.

Critical Reception

The single was noted for its laid-back style and creative structure.

Fan Reactions

The music video and lyrics sparked discussion and admiration for their layered meanings.
