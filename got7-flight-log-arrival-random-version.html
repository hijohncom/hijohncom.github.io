GOT7 / FLIGHT LOG: ARRIVAL (Random Version)

Embarking on a journey through sound and emotion, GOT7's 'Flight Log: Arrival' marks a pivotal moment in their musical expedition.

Album Overview

Released on March 13, 2017, 'Flight Log: Arrival' is the third and final installment in GOT7's 'Flight Log' series. The album features eight tracks, including the lead single 'Never Ever,' which blends future bass with R&B elements. This project showcases the group's evolution, combining experimental sounds with contemporary pop trends.

Critical Reception

Critics praised the album for its cohesive production and the group's vocal performances. The lead single 'Never Ever' was highlighted for its catchy hook and dynamic arrangement. Overall, the album was seen as a testament to GOT7's growth and versatility in the K-pop scene.

Fan Reactions

Fans expressed enthusiasm for the album's diverse tracklist and the group's musical experimentation. Many appreciated the emotional depth in songs like 'Paradise,' noting the blend of smooth vocals and engaging melodies.
